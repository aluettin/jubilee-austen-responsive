A little bit about me.

I started dabbling in code sixteen years ago and loved it so much, I earned a Webmaster Certification from St. Petersburg College. The subsequent decade found me raising four kids and earning a B.S. in the field of education. It wasn't until I started learning the ins and outs of WordPress a few years ago that I rekindled my love of coding. These days, I spend time creating, learning, and honing my skills on a daily basis and love every minute of it! When I'm not coding, you can find me hanging out with my family, DIYing something, or planning our next road trip.
